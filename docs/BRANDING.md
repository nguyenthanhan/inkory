# Inkory - Branding Guidelines

Official branding guidelines for **Inkory** - a modern blogging platform.

---

## 🎨 Brand Identity

### Official Name
**Inkory** - A modern blogging platform for writers and readers

### Tagline
"Share Your Stories, Connect With Readers"

### Description
Inkory is a modern, full-featured blogging platform that empowers writers to share their stories and readers to discover great content. Built with modern web technologies, Inkory provides a seamless experience for creating, sharing, and engaging with blog posts.

## 🎨 Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Dark (Black/Charcoal)
  - Hex: `#000000` or `#1F2937`
- **Secondary**: White
  - Hex: `#FFFFFF`
- **Accent**: Blue
  - Hex: `#3B82F6` (for highlights)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, modern sans-serif
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Code**: Monospace font

### Logo Concept
- Simple, modern design
- Represents "ink" or "writing"
- Scalable and recognizable

## 🌟 Brand Voice

### Tone
- Professional yet approachable
- Encouraging and supportive
- Clear and concise
- Helpful and informative

### Language
- Use "Inkory" (not "the Inkory" or "Inkory app")
- Use "writers" and "readers"
- Use "stories" instead of "posts"
- Use "share" instead of "publish"

### Examples
- ✅ "Share your story on Inkory"
- ✅ "Connect with readers on Inkory"
- ✅ "Discover great stories on Inkory"
- ❌ "Post on another platform"
- ❌ "Publish on the Inkory"
